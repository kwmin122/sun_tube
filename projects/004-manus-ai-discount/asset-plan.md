## Material Rules

- Capture is primary in scenes 02 and 06 and must be large enough to read.
- Zoom, highlight, and Korean labels are optional; narration and captions explain the capture.
- Imagegen is support only for thumbnail/mood and not required for body evidence.
- `video-use` is not required because there is no raw/interview/demo footage.
- No small source stamps.
