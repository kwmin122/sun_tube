# visual-essay/imagegen-cinematic-sequence

## Use When

- The generated image is the primary visual: symbolic story, future scenario, emotional hook, historical reenactment, or visual essay.

## Primary Routes

- `imagegen`

## Secondary Routes

- `hyperframes`
- `remotion`
- `kinetic-type`

## Required Primitives

- `liquidLens`
- `packetFlow` when symbolic elements move across scenes.

## Motion Beats

1. Establish image mood.
2. Push camera or parallax into the subject.
3. Reveal text as a timed thought, not a slide title.
4. Transition through image continuity or visual motif.

## Renderer Notes

- Use imagegen as `primary`, not a background afterthought.
- Do not put generated text inside the image.

## QA Fail If

- Same image holds too long without camera or semantic change.
- Image does not match narration emotion.
- Text overlays feel like a generic slide.

## Example Scenes

- History, future scenario, emotional story, visual essay intro.
