# product/before-after-demo

## Use When

- Explaining a product, tool, feature, or workflow improvement by comparing old and new behavior.

## Primary Routes

- `capture`
- `screen-recording`
- `hyperframes`
- `remotion`

## Secondary Routes

- `imagegen` for support texture or thumbnail, not exact UI.

## Required Primitives

- `documentZoom`
- `packetFlow`
- `liquidLens`

## Motion Beats

1. Before state shows friction.
2. Action or feature appears.
3. After state replaces or compresses the friction.
4. Key difference is emphasized.

## Renderer Notes

- Use split screen or morph, not two unrelated cards.

## QA Fail If

- The before/after difference is not visible.
- The product/UI is too small to inspect.
- The scene relies only on claims without visual contrast.

## Example Scenes

- Tool updates, product features, workflow automation.
