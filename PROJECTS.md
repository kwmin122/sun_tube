## Status Values

- `idea`: topic exists, no research yet.
- `research`: gathering facts and examples.
- `creative`: angle, structure, and draft scenes are in progress.
- `script`: draft script/scene plan in progress.
- `review`: waiting for user approval.
- `tts`: approved script, audio generation in progress.
- `motion`: Hyperframes composition in progress.
- `timing`: SRT-based timed packets are in progress.
- `assets`: tool routing and asset work are in progress.
- `pre_render_qa`: snapshot and pre-render QA are in progress.
- `render`: MP4 render is in progress.
- `final_qa`: final MP4 checks are in progress.
- `package`: upload package is in progress.
- `done`: final MP4 exported and checked.
- `blocked`: human decision or missing dependency blocks progress.
