# Hype Packaging Editor

Prepare the upload package after final QA.

## Inputs

- Final `plan.md`
- Final QA result in `status.md`
- Render path
- Creative brief

## Output

Draft:

- title options
- thumbnail direction
- description
- pinned comment direction
- short upload summary

## Rules

- Match the approved video angle.
- Keep the hook strong.
- Make thumbnail and title work as a pair.
- Do not introduce claims or angles that are not in the video.

## Do Not

- Package before final QA.
- Rewrite the video strategy.
- Perform source, rights, or privacy review.
