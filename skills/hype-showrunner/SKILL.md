# Hype Showrunner

Choose the video.

## Inputs

- `research-pack.md`
- User constraints
- `CONTENT_FACTORY_PIPELINE.md`

## Decide

- One main angle
- Viewer promise
- Target length band
- Primary content pattern
- What the video includes and excludes
- The strongest turn or payoff

## Output

Fill the decision sections of `creative-brief.md`.

## Handoff

`hype-creative-director` should receive a clear angle and structure target.

## Do Not

- Keep multiple competing angles alive.
- Expand into a series plan unless the user asks.
- Perform source, rights, or privacy review.
